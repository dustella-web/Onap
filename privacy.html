Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2025

ONAP ("the App") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos uploaded by users
  • Optional comments entered by users
  • Country information based on location permission (if granted)
  • Device identifiers and basic log data through Firebase
  • Anonymous advertising identifiers (AAID) for AdMob ads

2. Purpose of Collection

  • To enable photo sharing and storage
  • To provide localized experience based on country
  • To improve service stability and performance
  • To serve personalized ads through AdMob

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Uploaded photos and comments are securely stored in Firebase Firestore and Storage. Upon user request for account deletion or photo removal, associated data will be deleted following appropriate procedures.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties unless required by law or with the user's prior consent.

5. Advertising and Analytics

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (AAID), but ONAP does not directly store or access them.

6. User Rights

  • Users may request deletion of their personal information at any time.
  • Users can adjust ad personalization settings via their Google account.
